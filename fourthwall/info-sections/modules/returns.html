Are returns or exchanges accepted for LED jackets?

Only for defective products. Due to the made-to-order and highly customized nature of Knight Witch's Spellweave™ line, once we have begun the build process, we cannot issue refunds or accept returns. This, of course, excludes unexpected issues such as quality defects, faulty parts, etc. If you receive your order with any defects or a piece of the electronic hardware is not functioning as expected, please reach out & we will take care of it right away. We pack our products like we're expecting them to get drop-kicked by the courier, but things can happen once the package leaves our studio & is in the hands of the Shipping Gods.

Are returns or exchanges accepted for LED wall art?

For defective or damaged products only. However, some exceptions may allow returns or exchanges in specific cases outside damaged or defective orders. These are made to order and take quite a bit of time to craft, however if you are unsatisfied with your product, please don't hesitate to reach out. If it's an item we DO carry in stock, meaning one we have on-hand inventory of, we may be able to take a return or exchange, so it's always worth checking.
