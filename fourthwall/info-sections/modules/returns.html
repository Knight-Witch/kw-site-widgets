Made-To-Order Spellweave™ Apparel

Spellweave™ Apparel is made to order and may be customized for a specific jacket, size, design, hardware layout, and customer request. Once production has begun, orders are not eligible for cancellation, return, exchange, or refund due to change of mind, fit preference, event cancellation, or buyer's remorse.

If your order arrives with a defect, hardware issue, or quality problem, contact us within 7 days of delivery so we can review the issue. Eligible hardware and manufacturing issues may be handled through warranty support, replacement parts, repair, or another approved resolution.

Before Production Begins

If you need to change sizing, shipping details, garment selection, design details, or order timing, contact us as soon as possible. We can usually make changes before production begins. Once materials have been ordered, garments have been modified, electronics have been built, or design work has entered production, changes may no longer be possible or may require additional cost.

Made-To-Order Wall Art

Made-to-order LED wall art is not eligible for return, exchange, or refund once production begins unless it arrives damaged, defective, or not as described. If there is an issue with your wall art, contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and clear photos or video of the issue.

In-Stock Wall Art

In-stock wall art may be eligible for return or exchange if it is unused, undamaged, complete, and returned in approved packaging. Contact us before sending anything back. Returns sent without prior approval may be delayed or refused.

What To Report

If your order arrives damaged, defective, missing components, or with hardware that does not function as expected, contact us within 7 days of delivery. Include your order number, photos of the packaging, photos or video of the product issue, and a description of what happened.

Shipping Damage

Please keep the original packaging until the issue is resolved. Carrier claims may require photos of the box, packing material, label, and damaged item. If packaging is discarded before review, it may limit the options available for resolving the claim.

Resolution Options

Depending on the issue, we may repair the item, provide replacement parts, replace affected hardware, request return of a removable component, approve a replacement, or provide another appropriate resolution. Refunds are not the default remedy for custom or made-to-order work unless required by the specific circumstances.

Fit Questions

Contact us before production begins if you are unsure about sizing. We can help review fit, base-jacket selection, and measurements before your Spellweave™ build enters production.

Try Before You Buy

Try Before You Buy may be available for select jackets. Not all jackets are eligible, and custom sizes are excluded. If the jacket you want to try is unavailable, we will issue a refund for the Try Before You Buy item and contact you about next steps.

Custom Sizing

Custom sizing, altered garments, and special-order garments are final sale once production begins. Confirm measurements carefully before approving a custom or special-order build.
