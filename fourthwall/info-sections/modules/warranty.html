Warranty Overview

All Spellweave™ Apparel includes a 60-day limited warranty covering eligible defects in materials, workmanship, and electronic hardware. The warranty period begins on the date your order is delivered by the courier.

Each Spellweave™ build is tested before shipment to confirm electronic functionality, controller behavior, LED performance, and power compatibility. Your order may also include care instructions or build-specific details for your jacket, electronics, and removable Spellweave™ components.

This warranty is designed to cover unexpected defects, not damage caused by misuse, incompatible power hardware, improper care, modification, or normal wear. Damage that is not covered by warranty may still be eligible for paid repair through the Maintenance & Repair Program.

Covered Issues

  • Unexpected Bluetooth controller failure, including failure to power on or appear in device search.
  • LED, wiring, or connector failure not caused by visible physical damage or misuse.
  • Included power bank or approved power accessory failure.
  • Sewing, zipper, attachment, or garment construction defects caused by manufacturing error.
  • Other identifiable defects in materials, workmanship, or assembly that are not caused by customer damage.

Not Covered

  • Cosmetic wear, scratches, dents, scuffs, fading, abrasion, or expected aging from normal use.
  • Crush damage, impact damage, punctures, tears, burns, melted components, or damage from unsafe storage or travel.
  • Water damage caused by submersion, washing with electronics installed, improper drying, opened housings, damaged seals, or failure to follow care instructions.
  • Electrical damage caused by incompatible power banks, chargers, adapters, cables, voltage, current, wiring, connectors, or power delivery behavior.
  • Damage caused by tampering, modification, disassembly, unapproved repair attempts, opened electronics, or altered Spellweave™ components.
  • Loss, theft, accidental disposal, or damage caused by pets, children, baggage handling, venues, security checks, or third-party handling after delivery.
  • Fit preference, change of mind, event cancellation, buyer's remorse, or preference changes after production begins.

Rain & Moisture

Spellweave™ electronics are built for normal rain exposure when used according to the care guide. Rain-safe does not mean submersible, washable with electronics installed, or immune to damage from improper drying or opened housings.

If your jacket gets wet, remove the electronic components as instructed and allow the jacket and Spellweave™ components to dry fully before storage or reuse. Do not store wet electronics in a sealed bag, pile, luggage compartment, or closed garment pocket.

Power Compatibility

Use of a third-party power source does not automatically void your warranty. However, damage caused by incompatible voltage, current, connectors, wiring, adapters, chargers, or power delivery behavior is not covered.

If you want to use a different power bank, charger, cable, splitter, or adapter, contact us before use. We can review compatibility and help you avoid avoidable damage. Compatibility review does not extend warranty coverage to unrelated misuse, unsafe wiring, improper charging, or later modification.

Modification & Repair Attempts

Do not open, cut into, rewire, reseal, solder, modify, or attempt to repair the Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™ or related electronics unless Knight Witch has provided written approval for that specific action. Damage caused by unauthorized modification or repair attempts is not covered.

What To Send

To start a warranty claim, contact us through the site form or email info@KnightWitchApparel.com. Include your order number, the name on the order, a description of the issue, and clear photos or video showing the problem.

Review Process

  • We review the issue based on the information you provide.
  • We may ask for additional photos, video, troubleshooting steps, or confirmation of the power hardware being used.
  • If the issue is covered, we will determine whether the best solution is repair, replacement parts, replacement hardware, or inspection of the affected Spellweave™ component.
  • If the issue is not covered, we can provide a paid repair estimate through the Maintenance & Repair Program.

Shipping

If a covered warranty repair requires shipment, Knight Witch will provide instructions before anything is sent in. Covered domestic warranty repairs may include a prepaid shipping label when return shipment is required. International warranty shipping, customs fees, import taxes, and carrier limitations may require separate handling.

Do not mail a jacket, Cauldron Core™, controller, battery, cable, or accessory to us without receiving instructions first. Unapproved shipments may be delayed, returned, or ineligible for processing.

Care Plan Overview

The Spellweave™ Care Plan is an optional service plan that provides extended support benefits, eligible repair discounts, and maintenance-related service options for twelve months from delivery, plus select Maintenance & Repair Program discounts for three years from the original purchase date.

The Care Plan does not replace or expand every term of the 60-Day Limited Warranty. It provides the additional service benefits listed below.

Included Benefits

  • Priority review for eligible troubleshooting, support, repair, and maintenance requests.
  • Extended service support for eligible hardware issues during the first twelve months after delivery.
  • A 30% discount on eligible non-covered repair work during the 12-month Care Plan period, excluding full jacket replacement unless explicitly approved.
  • A 20% discount on eligible Maintenance & Repair Program work for three years from the original purchase date.

Not Included

  • Full jacket replacement unless Knight Witch specifically approves replacement as the appropriate remedy.
  • Loss, theft, cosmetic wear, fit preference, buyer's remorse, or change of mind.
  • Damage caused by misuse, submersion, incompatible power hardware, unsafe storage, unauthorized modification, disassembly, or unapproved repair attempts.
  • Shipping costs, customs fees, duties, taxes, or carrier-related charges unless Knight Witch confirms otherwise for a specific covered service request.

After The Care Plan Ends

When the Care Plan period ends, you may still request repair, refresh, inspection, replacement components, or hardware upgrades through the Maintenance & Repair Program. Paid repairs are quoted before work begins.
