Basic Overview

All Spellweave™ Apparel is covered by a 60-day warranty as part of your jacket’s quality assurance package. This warranty period begins from the point you receive your jacket from the courier, therefore you will have 60 days with your jacket in use before you exit the standard warranty period.

Each jacket build is put through Q&A testing to confirm functionality of its electronic components and a long lasting battery life. With each build, we include the specs unique to your jacket, as well as technical procedures regarding care & maintenance of Spellweave™ apparel for a long & vibrant life.

With that said, in the event of an unexpected hardware failure, we’ve got you covered. The standard warranty will cover faulty hardware issues, however, there are some exceptions that come down to user-error. It is imperative that you read the user guide & care procedures in order to ensure you do not unintentionally damage your jacket’s electronic hardware. Failure to adhere to these details may result in damage that is not covered. However, we also offer a Maintenance & Repair Program available to you at any time, so ANY damage not covered by your warranty can almost always be repaired and / or refreshed as part of the M&RP.

What Is Covered

  • Unexpected hardware failure
  • Manufacturing defects or quality issues
  • Power source failure

What Is NOT Covered

  • Water damage
  • Crush damage
  • Fried electronics
  • Scratches / general expected wear & tear
  • Tampering, modification, & any unapproved meddling with your hardware & / or Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™.

The following damage IS covered by the Standard 60-day Warranty:

  • Unexpected bluetooth controller failure such as not powering on or not found in device search.

What Is NOT Covered

The following damage is NOT covered by your warranty:

  • Water damage. Jackets are water-tight but not submersible, so stay on land. Rain is OK.
  • Crush damage. They are durable, but they still contain electronic hardware, so don’t falcon-punch it into your luggage or you’re gonna have a bad time.
  • Fried electronics.
  • Scratches / general expected wear & tear.
  • Tampering, modification, & any unapproved meddling with your hardware & / or Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™.

Fried Electronics - Please Read

As outlined in the user guide and care procedures, the electronic components in the Spellweave™ Apparel builds are HEAVILY tested for compatibility.

Do NOT under ANY circumstances use an alternative power source from the power bank provided. You run a very high chance of frying your electronics.

The behaviours from receiving too much power are VERY obvious. In our engineering process, it was a challenge to find a configuration that met our standards, as well as compatibility, as we found some hardware that should have worked on paper did NOT in fact play nicely together. We went through this so you do not have to.

If you want to try out an alternative power source / bank, please simply reach out to us first, and we can complete a testing process to ensure it will not compromise your jacket build. Once we do this, we will send the approval and add a note to your account that your alternative power source was APPROVED by QA.

Warranty Repair Process

In the event your jacket presents any unexpected issues, please reach out to us either using the form on KnightWitchApparel.com, or email us at info@KnightWitchApparel.com with your order number & details on the issue you are experiencing. Repairs covered will be done at no cost to you, and if it is necessary to send your jacket in for repair, we will provide you with a shipping label at no extra cost. Most repairs will not require the entire jacket to be sent in. In the case of faulty external hardware, such as controller, battery, or cables, we will send you replacement parts. In the event the Spellweave™ design has an internal issue, typically we will just require the Spellweave™ Cauldron Core™ to be removed from your jacket and sent in for repair.

PLEASE UNDERSTAND THAT TAMPERING WITH YOUR Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™ WILL AUTOMATICALLY VOID YOUR WARRANTY.

We cannot cover damage incurred if your Cauldron Core™ has been opened or modified because we have no way of determining if the issue was a result of bad ideas, or a manufacturing defect. We are very serious about providing a long-lasting product & want to ensure we can cover you as much as possible. As creators and tinkerers, we understand some may wish to modify their builds and simply must warn you to do so at your own risk. If you are ever curious to make changes to your jacket, please just reach out to us first and we can likely help you and provide some insight to ensure you don’t accidentally cause a singularity that will swallow the planet whole, summon Cthulhu, or damage your jacket.
