Basic Overview

All Spellweave™ Apparel is covered by a 60-day warranty as part of your jacket’s quality assurance package. This warranty period begins from the point you receive your jacket from the courier, so you will have 60 days with your jacket in use before you exit the standard warranty period.

Each jacket build is put through QA testing to confirm functionality of its electronic components and battery life. With each build, we include the specs unique to your jacket, as well as technical procedures regarding care and maintenance of Spellweave™ apparel.

In the event of an unexpected hardware failure, the standard warranty covers faulty hardware issues. Some exceptions come down to user error. You must read the user guide and care procedures to ensure you do not unintentionally damage your jacket’s electronic hardware. Failure to adhere to these details may result in damage that is not covered. Damage not covered by warranty can almost always be repaired or refreshed through the Maintenance & Repair Program.

What Is Covered

  • Unexpected hardware failure
  • Manufacturing defects or quality issues
  • Power source failure

What Is NOT Covered

  • Water damage
  • Crush damage
  • Fried electronics
  • Scratches and general expected wear and tear
  • Tampering, modification, and any unapproved meddling with your hardware or Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™

Included for ALL Spellweave™ Apparel

The following damage is covered by the Standard 60-day Warranty:

  • Unexpected Bluetooth controller failure such as not powering on or not being found in device search
  • Wiring or LED node failure unless visibly caused by physical damage
  • Power bank or power source failure such as being unable to charge, hold charge, or power properly
  • Fraying, sewing issues, or broken zipper hardware due to manufacturing defect
  • Any general identifiable manufacturing defects or issues not caused by user error

What Is NOT Covered

The following damage is not covered by your warranty:

  • Water damage
  • Crush damage
  • Fried electronics
  • Scratches and general expected wear and tear
  • Tampering, modification, and any unapproved meddling with your hardware or Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™

In the event your jacket presents any unexpected issues, reach out through KnightWitchApparel.com or email info@KnightWitchApparel.com with your order number and details on the issue you are experiencing. Repairs covered by warranty will be done at no cost to you.

Tampering with your Spellweave™ LED Cauldron Core™ automatically voids your warranty.

Duration

  • 12 months from the date your jacket is delivered

Extended Warranty Overview

As part of the long-life mission to bring you Spellweave™ Apparel that lasts, we offer an extended and expanded warranty to cover the first year of ownership. Your warranty includes damage coverage that is not part of the standard warranty, a longer coverage timeframe, and additional Maintenance & Repair Program discounts after warranty expiration.

This warranty grants:

  • Expanded coverage for additional hardware failures not covered by the standard warranty
  • 12-month failure coverage, upgraded from the 60-day standard coverage
  • A discount on repair and maintenance requests

The Spellweave™ Apparel Maintenance & Repair Program gives customers a reasonable option to maintain Spellweave™ jackets for many years and seek repairs when damage is incurred. This includes Spellweave™ Cauldron Core™ replacements and upgrade opportunities when newer hardware becomes available.
