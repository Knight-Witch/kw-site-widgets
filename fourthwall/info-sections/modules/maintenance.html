Program Overview

The Spellweave™ Maintenance & Repair Program exists so your jacket can be serviced, refreshed, repaired, or upgraded after purchase. Warranty coverage handles eligible defects during the warranty period. Maintenance and repair support remains available after warranty expiration and for many issues that are not covered by warranty.

You can request a repair review at any time. Some damage may not be practical or cost-effective to repair, but we will review the issue and explain the available options before any paid work begins.

Common Service Requests

  • Spellweave™ face refreshes for scratches, dents, scuffs, or worn exterior surfaces.
  • LED, wiring, connector, or cable repairs.
  • Bluetooth controller, power accessory, or removable hardware replacement.
  • Cauldron Core™ inspection, repair, or replacement.
  • Attachment, pocket, zipper, seam, or garment construction repairs where repair is feasible.
  • Hardware upgrades when newer components are compatible with your build.

Start With Photos Or Video

To begin, submit the repair form or contact info@KnightWitchApparel.com. Include your order number if available, a description of the issue, and clear photos or video showing the damaged or malfunctioning area.

Initial Review

We will review the information you provide and give an initial assessment. If the issue appears covered by warranty, we will process it as a warranty claim. If the issue is outside warranty coverage, we will provide the next steps for a paid repair estimate.

Final Estimate

Initial estimates are based on the photos, video, and information provided. The final cost may change after physical inspection if hidden damage, additional component failure, contamination, unsafe modification, or unrelated damage is found. We will not begin paid repair work without approval of the estimate.

Repair Limits

Most Spellweave™ components are designed to be serviceable, but some damage may prevent repair or make replacement more practical than restoration.

  • Severe crush damage or structural deformation.
  • Burned, melted, contaminated, moldy, or chemically damaged materials.
  • Damage from incompatible power hardware, unsafe wiring, or unapproved electrical modification.
  • Opened, cut, punctured, resealed, rewired, or heavily altered electronics.
  • Missing components required to complete the repair.
  • Garments that are too damaged, contaminated, or unstable to safely work on.

If a repair is not feasible, we may be able to quote replacement components, a partial rebuild, or a new Spellweave™ installation depending on the original design and available materials.

Shipping

Do not ship anything to Knight Witch until you receive repair instructions. Some issues can be resolved with replacement parts or removable components, and the full jacket may not need to be sent in.

For non-warranty repairs, customers are generally responsible for inbound shipping. Return shipping may be included in the estimate or billed separately depending on the repair, destination, and shipment requirements. Covered warranty repairs follow the warranty shipping rules.

Repair Timeline

Repair timelines depend on inspection needs, component availability, production queue, shipping time, and the complexity of the work. We will provide an estimated timeframe after reviewing the repair request. Rush requests may be possible but are not guaranteed.
