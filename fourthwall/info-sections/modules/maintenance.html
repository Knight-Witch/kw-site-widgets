Overview

All Spellweave™ Apparel can be sent in for repairs & maintenance at any time. While certain damage is covered by your warranty, we want to make sure your jacket lasts long after the warranty period has ended. If you need to refresh your jacket's decal face from scratches, dents, etc, or fix a hole you accidently jammed through it trying to find your chapstick too aggressively, we can fix it.

How To Send In For Repairs

Fill out the form below to get started. Let us know what you need done, and include your order number in your request so we can apply eligible warranty coverage and/or your Maintenance and Repair discount accordingly. Please provide photos showing damage or any details that will help us assess what your jacket needs.

Not sure what you need?

That's okay. Fill out the form to start and we will walk you through the process.

Can I send my jacket in to repair even if my warranty is expired?

Yes. You can always send it in for repairs, refreshers, and hardware upgrades. This program exists to make sure that whether it has been five years or five months since you purchased a Spellweave™ product, it can last for the long term. Some things may prevent repairs from being feasible, but most damage can be fixed.

What's the cost associated with it?

That depends on the work it needs done. Fill out the form with the relevant details and we will provide an estimate. Payment plans are available for purchases through Afterpay.
