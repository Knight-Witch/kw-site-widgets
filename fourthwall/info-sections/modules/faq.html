Are Spellweave™ LED jackets safe for the rain?

YES! All Spellweave™ Apparel designs have received the official status of Bass Canyon 2024 Thicc Rain & Thunderstorm Seal of Approval, meaning you can wear these through a rainstorm & shouldn't experience any issues. That being said:

  • Not all base-jackets themselves are ideal for rain. Some genuine leather jackets are not treated to endure a downpour, so please refer to your jacket's Care Guide or reach out to us if you aren't sure. Each Care Guide is specific to the base-jacket your Spellweave™ design is installed on, so we take an extra effort to make sure your guide is specific to your build.
  • We DO recommend once you shed your jacket to remove the electronic components & hang your jacket to dry to help ensure it remains as weatherproofed as possible for many years to come.
  • They are not submersible on a level that makes swimming in your electronic apparel a good idea, so please stay on land or you're gonna have a BAD time.
