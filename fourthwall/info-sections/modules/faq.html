Are Spellweave™ LED jackets safe for the rain?

Yes. Spellweave™ Apparel designs are built to tolerate rain, but they are not submersible. Base jackets may have their own care limits, especially genuine leather jackets, so customers should refer to the Care Guide specific to their build.

  • Not all base jackets are ideal for heavy rain.
  • After wearing in rain, remove the electronic components and hang the jacket to dry.
  • Do not swim in electronic apparel.

Are returns or exchanges accepted for LED jackets?

Returns and exchanges are accepted for defective products. Due to the made-to-order and customized nature of Spellweave™ apparel, refunds and returns are not available once the build process has begun unless there is a defect, quality issue, or faulty component.

Are returns or exchanges accepted for LED wall art?

For LED wall art, returns and exchanges are primarily for defective or damaged products. Some exceptions may be possible for stocked inventory, but made-to-order pieces are handled case by case.
